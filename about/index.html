About Me

 Hello Key Clubbers!

My name is Devin Sun, and I am from the New Jersey District of Key Club International. This past service year, I had the pleasure of serving as a Lieutenant Governor. From the experiences that I gained as a Lieutenant Governor, I knew that I had more to give to the wonderful organization that is Key Club International.

AS A KEY CLUBBER

I was introduced to Key Club at a young age. My older brother was in high school while I was in middle school. He would always come home speaking about some club that he was involved in. I did not think much of it, since he was always involved in so many different activities. One day, however, I remember being told by my mother that we were invited as guests to a District Convention. At that time, I, of course, had no idea what a District Convention was. As I arrived, I saw hundreds of high school students, sitting in one enormous ballroom, all carefully watching a girl speaking at a podium. As my mother and I took our seats, I watched in awe, at the spectacle that is Key Club. I saw the passion for service in each Key Clubber. I saw the kindness in each Key Clubber. And, ultimately, I saw a will to change the world for the better. It was at that precise moment, that I knew that I wanted to become a Key Clubber.

AS A HIGH SCHOOLER

Like you all, I'm just like any other normal student. I have homework, I like to hang out with friends, and I have hobbies outside of school. As for extracurriculars aside from Key Club, I am in my school's Forensics Speech & Debate Team, FBLA, and French Honor Society. Some of my hobbies include longboarding, fishing, and fencing. My favorite subject in school would either be math or science.

QUESTIONS
